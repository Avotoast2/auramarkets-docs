The [Single Names](https://auramarkets.io/single-names) section is where you leave the macro view and zoom into individual tickers. Build watchlists of names you actually care about, scan sectors for what's moving, run screens for specific setups.

## Three views

The Single Names section has three sub-pages, switchable from the **Single Names ▾** dropdown in the top nav:

## Watchlist controls

The top of the Watchlists page exposes these controls:

- **Watchlist picker** — pick from your saved lists, or create a new one (`+`)
- **Edit / Delete** — manage the active list
- **Browse Sectors** — load any sector as a watchlist, optionally filtered by:
  - Industry (sub-sector)
  - Market cap (Mega / Large / Mid / Small)
  - Include ETFs

This means you can switch between curated lists ("my AI names," "energy infra") and on-demand sector loads ("show me all mid-cap healthcare") without leaving the page.

## What's available beyond watchlists

- **Phase-change screen** — flags tickers that just changed regime (trend, vol, breadth). → [Phase change](/single-names/phase-change)
- **Heatmaps** — color-encoded grids of your watchlist over the active timeframe. → [Watchlists & heatmaps](/single-names/watchlists-heatmaps)
- **Watchlist history** — track how your list has performed over time
- **Scatter views** — Risk/Return, Drawdown/Recovery, Tail Risk, Capture Ratio, Sharpe Stability (in the screener)

## How most people use it

1. **Start with sectors** — Browse Sectors → load a sector → glance at the heatmap for outliers
2. **Build a personal watchlist** — drop in the 30–50 names you actually trade or follow
3. **Check phase-change daily** — flagged tickers are where attention should go
4. **Use the Screener** — when you have a specific setup in mind ("show me all stocks down −2σ over 1W")
