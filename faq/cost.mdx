---
title: "What does it cost?"
description: "Free during the friends-and-family phase."
---

**Free.** During the current friends-and-family phase, auramarkets has no billing and no plans.

## What that means in practice

- No credit card required to sign up
- No usage limits beyond what's reasonable for personal use
- No "premium tier" gatekeeping features

## What might change

Eventually billing will exist. The likely shape:

- A **free tier** that covers most of what's free today
- A **paid tier** for higher-frequency / paid data feeds, more research integrations, and faster updates
- Existing friends-and-family users will be informed in advance with reasonable migration paths

No specific date or pricing yet. When it changes, you'll hear about it in advance.

## Until then

Use it freely, give feedback, break things, send bug reports.
