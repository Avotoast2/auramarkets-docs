auramarkets is a personal markets dashboard. It pulls together the things I find myself checking every day — macro signals, single-name research, my own portfolio's risk — in one place, dark and dense, no fluff.

Right now it's free and invite-only. If you're reading this, you probably know me — let me know what you think.

## A few notes

- **Data lag.** Right now most data comes from free feeds (yfinance), delayed roughly 15–20 minutes. The plan is to move to near-real-time paid feeds once the site is more bootstrapped. For now, use auramarkets for context, not execution.
- **Active development.** Features ship fast and occasionally need polish — your feedback is the fastest way to make the next version better.
- **One developer.** Built by me. Send bugs, ideas, and feature requests straight to me.
