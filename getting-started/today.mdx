[Today](https://auramarkets.io/today) is where auramarkets opens. It's the synthesis page: research coverage, tracked calls, 13F flows, earnings, macro events, and unusual price action — intersected with **your** portfolio and watchlists, refreshed every ~15 minutes.

## Your names

The centerpiece. Every name you follow (portfolio + watchlists) that had something happen recently gets a row — logo, ticker, company, the day's move with its σ-context, and **pills explaining exactly why it surfaced**:

| Pill | Meaning |
|---|---|
| <span style={{color:"#5fd68f"}}>**LONG·TA**</span> / <span style={{color:"#ef7a78"}}>**SHORT·FA**</span> | A fresh tracked call — green long / red short, tagged by what grounds it: **TA** (technicals), **FA** (fundamentals), or **MIXED**. When a name shows TA bears against FA bulls, that split *is* the signal. Pills also carry the writer's [grade](/research/calls#writer-grades): a **gold ring** = FOLLOW writer, a **dimmed ⚠** = FADE writer (contrary indicator). Hover for the evidence line — "FOLLOW · 21d avg +4.6% vs SPY · 61% hit · n=134". |
| <span style={{color:"#a78bfa"}}>**FLIP**</span> | A writer **reversed a standing position** — "Zephyr: long→short · was +32% over 4mo". Flips rank names near the top, and arguably it's the strongest single signal on the page: someone with a public position just changed their mind. |
| **X / SUBSTACK / YOUTUBE** | A dedicated piece by one of your writers (grouped per author — "Zephyr ×3"). Dimmed **MENTION** pills are passing references. |
| **13F** | Cluster funds bought or sold it last quarter. |
| **EARNINGS** | Reports today (before open / after close). |
| **MOVE** | A ±2σ-or-bigger day *for that name's own volatility*. |
| <span style={{color:"#5fd68f"}}>**52W HIGH**</span> / <span style={{color:"#ef7a78"}}>**52W LOW**</span>, **↑200D / ↓200D** | Major levels only: a fresh 52-week extreme, or a cross of the 200-day — the event, not the standing state. A quiet name that just broke out surfaces here even with zero coverage. |

Rows are ranked by how much happened; the first five pills show and the rest sit behind a **+N more** expander.

**Hover any pill** for a preview card — for calls, the *verbatim sentence* where the writer made the call; for coverage, the piece's extracted thesis (or the full tweet). Every card links both ways: **auramarkets →** (the article page) and the original (**open on X ↗**, Substack, YouTube).

**Click any ticker** and the full [stock pop-out](/indices/stock-chart) opens right on the page — chart with call/earnings/13F markers, studies, compare, company panel, Ask Aura — close it and you're back where you were.

**Search** works like the Calls search: type to filter live, Enter stacks a tag (AND semantics), and terms match tickers, company names, writers, and piece titles. A **suggestion dropdown** offers matches from your own rows as you type — tickers with company names, and the writers attached to them (with how many of your names each one touched) — pick one to commit it as a tag.

## The rest of the page

- **Writer signal** — first-ever corpus mentions, new deep-dives, fresh calls, and cross-writer consensus, each track scrolling independently with its item count. Tickers show their **logo and company name** (same look as Your names), and **hovering any deep-dive or fresh-call item** pops the same preview card as the pills above — the piece's thesis, or the call quoted verbatim, with both links.
- **Smart money** — the 13F cluster consensus: per name, logo, the 1-day move, a buyer/seller bar, and the net dollar flow across funds.
- **On deck** — today's macro releases with tier badges and actual / consensus / previous, plus who reports earnings (logos inline, your held names starred ★).
- **Rotation watch** — quadrant-crossing events from the [rotation view](/indices/thematic-heatmap#rotation-view): themes **newly leading ↗**, **turning up ⤴**, or **rolling over ↘** vs SPY this week. These are *crossings*, not standings — a theme only appears when it just changed quadrant. Click through to the rotation view to see the full picture.
- **Market color** — the live overnight tape (ES/NQ futures, DXY, 10Y, gold, WTI, BTC), yesterday's closes, and the full **Market Pulse** briefing rendered by the same component as the macro page.
- **Regime badge** — the current market state, top right, sourced from the [regime engine](/macro/market-pulse#the-regime-badge) (not AI sentiment). Hover for the confidence % and the ranked anomalies behind the read.
- **30-day regime strip** — right under the badge: one colored cell per trading day showing the engine's state over the past month. Hover any cell for that day's state — a glance tells you whether today's read is a fresh flip or week three of the same regime.

## Time travel

The **◀ ▶ arrows** by the date step through archived snapshots — each day's final payload is preserved, so you can re-read any past morning exactly as it stood (history accumulates from July 3, 2026). The overnight tape hides on archived days, since it's inherently live.
