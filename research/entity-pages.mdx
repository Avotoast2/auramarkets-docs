The **entity page** for any ticker (e.g., `/research/entity/NVDA`) is a single timeline of every research item that has mentioned that name across Substack, X, and YouTube.

Useful when you're about to make a decision on a name and want to know what the smart-money writers and operators have been saying — without having to remember which Substack newsletter covered it last month.

## How to get there

Three ways:

1. **Click any ticker badge** in any feed card — auto-navigates to the entity page
2. **Direct URL** — `auramarkets.io/research/entity/NVDA`
3. **From the Single Names modal** — when you click a ticker in your watchlist, the side panel includes a research tab

## What's on the page

### Header
- Ticker, company name, current price, 1D / 1W / 1M change
- Recent macro context for the name (sector, industry, market cap)

### Timeline of mentions
Every piece of research that mentioned this ticker, sorted by recency by default.

For each mention:
- Source (Substack / X / YouTube)
- Author + publication
- Snippet showing the context the ticker appeared in
- Confidence badge
- Whether it was a **primary** mention (the piece was *about* this name) or **mentioned** (passing reference)

### Filters
- **Primary only** — hide passing references. Useful when a name shows up in 50 macro pieces but you only want the 5 deep-dives.
- **Confidence floor** — hide low-confidence noise.
- **Date range** — last 30 / 90 / 365 days, or custom.

### Aggregate view
A small panel showing:
- **Mention count** over time (sparkline) — useful for spotting attention spikes
- **Sentiment shift** if detectable
- **Top authors** who've covered this name most frequently

## How to use it

- **Pre-trade research** — before adding a position, hit the entity page. 10 minutes of skimming the last 30 days will tell you what the bull and bear cases are.
- **Track attention** — when mention count spikes, something's brewing. The sparkline is a thin signal but useful.
- **Find your authors** — the "top authors" panel surfaces who covers this name well. Follow them on Substack/X if you don't already.
