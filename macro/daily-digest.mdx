The **Daily Digest** is a morning email that arrives at **6:30am ET on weekdays** with a cross-feature roll-up of what's worth knowing before the open. It's the same data you'd see across the app, but pre-filtered for the things touching your portfolio and watchlists.

Subject line preview: *"AuraMarkets Daily — 30Y at 18-year high; NVDA in your portfolio +2.1% overnight; Hound Partners initiates MSFT"*

## What's in it

The email has five sections, in this order:

### 1. Today's read (synthesis)

The top block. A handful of cross-feature joins that matter for *your* book specifically:
- **In your portfolio** — names you hold that got new analyst coverage, new 13F flows, or notable overnight moves
- **On radar** — names on your watchlists with the same triggers

The synthesis is opinionated. It tries to surface the 3-5 items that aren't just "things happened" but "things happened that you should care about given what you own."

### 2. Markets

The headline macro line — equity index moves, regime, the Pulse key-signal commentary including any multi-year or 52-week extremes (e.g., *"30Y yield at 5.05%, highest since Aug 2007"*).

### 3. Portfolio

- Your overnight P&L
- Top 3 movers in your portfolio (and watchlists)
- Names with earnings today

### 4. 13F watch

Recent filings from tracked managers — who initiated what, who exited what — filtered to names overlapping your portfolio or watchlists where possible.

### 5. Writers

- Articles ingested overnight from your Substack/YouTube/X corpus, surfaced for relevance
- "Chatter" — names with unusual coverage volume

## Settings

Currently the digest is opt-in via your profile (coming soon — the toggle isn't wired into UI yet). For now, message support if you want to be added.

The send address is `digest@send.auramarkets.io` (custom domain on Resend). Add to your contacts so it doesn't end up in promotions.

## Cadence

| When | What |
|---|---|
| **6:30am ET weekdays** | Daily digest |
| **No send weekends** | Holiday calendar respected |
| **Pre-market data** | Snapshots taken at ~6:15am ET, so prices reflect overnight futures/extended-hours |

## What it's not

- **Not a real-time alert system** — for that, use [Market Pulse](/macro/market-pulse) right rail
- **Not personalized commentary** — the synthesis is data-driven, not opinionated about whether to buy/sell
- **Not exhaustive** — it surfaces the top items per section, not every event
