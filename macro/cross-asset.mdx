The **Cross-Asset Scanner** is the customizable workspace for everything *not* equity-index — rates, FX, credit, commodities, sector ETFs, anything you want to keep an eye on without cluttering the main views.

Think of it as your personal Bloomberg launch page, dark.

## What's in a tile

Each tile shows:

- **Ticker + name**
- **Current price / level**
- **Change (1D, configurable)**
- **Sparkline** over the active timeframe
- **Percentile shading** (1Y) — same color logic as the [Indices cards](/indices/reading-cards)
- **Sigma badge** if the reading is at an extreme

Click any tile to open a detail chart with full history.

## What's available

The scanner catalog includes (non-exhaustive):

- **Rates** — 2Y, 5Y, 10Y, 30Y US Treasuries; 2s10s, 3m10y spreads; SOFR; international 10Y yields
- **FX** — DXY, major crosses (EURUSD, USDJPY, GBPUSD, USDCNH), EM crosses
- **Credit** — HYG, LQD, JNK, EMB, IG/HY spread proxies
- **Commodities** — WTI, Brent, gold, silver, copper, natural gas, agricultural softs
- **Crypto** — BTC, ETH, total market cap
- **Sector ETFs** — XLF, XLK, XLE, XLU, XLV, etc.

If a ticker is on yfinance, it can probably be added.

## Customizing your scanner

The scanner remembers your selection per-account.

- **Add a tile** — pick from the catalog, drag into your scanner
- **Remove a tile** — drag it out, or click the × in edit mode
- **Reorder** — drag to reorganize
- **Saves automatically** — no "save" button; your changes persist

## How most people use it

- **Build a "regime watch" board** — DXY, 10Y, HYG, gold, BTC. When all five move together, regime is shifting.
- **Build a "single asset deep dive" board** — focus on oil with crude curve, refining margins, USD, and inventory-sensitive equities side by side.
- **Build a "morning checks" board** — overnight S&P futures, DXY, 10Y, BTC, gold for a 30-second pre-open scan.
